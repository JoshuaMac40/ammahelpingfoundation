About AHF

AHF is an idea from a bunch of youngsters from India for a great cause. They formed as a group and voluntarily started serving the poor and needy homeless people around their city.

They raised donations for the treatment of specially abled children and children who needs medical attention.

Ocassionaly they visit ophonages to spend some time with them.
